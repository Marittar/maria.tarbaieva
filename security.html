One of the Problems to solve

Team I am working in is focused on self service and enabling customers reach their goals without help of CSMs and Sales. There where no seamless transition of information between multiple user personas that caused missunderstandings and missaligments.

Design sprint

A design sprint is a powerful tool for teams to work towards a shared vision to design and test features quickly. As a globally distributed organisation, Cobalt quickly adapted to COVID-19 by becoming a remote-first company. As part of that process, I adapted design thinking best practices to the new normal-virtual office and created design sprint structure that fits organisation needs

This format worked really well in getting a sprint team of cross-functional stakeholders on the same page, generating innovative ideas, and gathering detailed insights from each member.
